INTERNSHIP

I completed my internship for my degree at Qubix, a company part of the Cronos Group that specializes in developing solutions using the Microsoft Power Platform. From February 2024 to May 2024, my task was to develop a PCF (PowerApps Component Framework) component for an internal project, designed to facilitate work registration for consultants.

As being one of the consultants myself, I understood the challenges and requirements of the work registration process. Knowing this component would be used by my colleagues and me made the project more meaningful and motivating.

The PCF component I developed is integrated into an existing application used by our consultants. Its primary function is to provide a better overview of existing registrations and streamline the creation of new ones, saving time and reducing errors.

Throughout the development process, I ensured the component was user-friendly and met our consultants' specific needs. I collaborated closely with colleagues to gather feedback and make necessary adjustments, resulting in an effective and efficient final product.

Overall, this internship allowed me to apply my technical skills in a real-world setting and gain valuable insights into the practical needs of consultants. It was a rewarding experience that emphasized the importance of developing functional and user-centric solutions.
